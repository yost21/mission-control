## PART B: LIVING WILL (DECLARATION OF WISHES)

### IV. When This Living Will Applies

This Living Will applies ONLY when I am in one of the following conditions, as
certified by my attending physician and one additional physician:

1. **Terminal Condition** - An incurable condition that will result in death
   within a relatively short time
2. **Persistent Vegetative State** - A permanent and irreversible condition of
   unconsciousness with no awareness of myself or my surroundings
3. **End-Stage Condition** - An irreversible condition caused by injury,
   disease, or illness that has resulted in severe and permanent deterioration

## IMPORTANT REMINDERS

1. **Give copies** to your Healthcare Agent, alternate agent, doctor(s), local
   hospital, and close family members
2. **Keep the original** in a safe, accessible place (NOT locked away)
3. **Review periodically** and update if your wishes change
4. **Carry a card** in your wallet stating that you have an Advance Directive
   and where it can be found
5. **For DNR specifically**: Ask your doctor about a POLST/MOLST form (Physician
   Orders for Life-Sustaining Treatment) - this is a medical order that
   emergency responders can follow immediately
6. **State registry**: Some states have Advance Directive registries where you
   can file your document for easy access
